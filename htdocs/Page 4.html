Isko Moreno

Name: Francisco Moreno Domagoso
Name on Ballot: Isko Moreno Domagoso
Party: Aksyon Demokratiko
Current Government Position: Mayor of Manila

In a somewhat surprising action, the 3-year Mayor of Manila decided to announce his bid to the top of Philippine politics. His uprbinging in the slums and populist rhetoric made him a popular figure among the less fortunate of our country, especially those from Manila, where he has implemented a massive infrastructure development program over the course of his mayoral tenure. He seeks to replicate his successes in the capital over a national level, not to dissimilar from our current president Duterte's bid back in 2016, this includes more housing, better healthcare systems, and more schools. Vice-presidential candidate Willie Ong was an odd choice, but highlights their campaign goal of coping with the pandemic and giving proper service to those who can't afford healthcare.

"Totoo po ang sinasabi nila. Sige na, sabihin na nila nagmamadali tayo. Eh dapat lang, 39 years na kami naghintay eh"
-Moreno, talking about the administrations of the Marcoses and Aquinos

He remains a popular candidate, tied at 3rd with fellow candidate Manny Pacquiao; however, many still question his seeming eagerness to become president after only a few years as actual mayor.
